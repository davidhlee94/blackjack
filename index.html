Rules of BlackJack

1. New game deals 2 cards to the dealer and the player.

2. Points totaling 21 win the game.

3. If total of 21 is not reached, the person with total closest to 21 wins.

4. Hit will add an additional card to hand.

5. Stand keeps your hand and total. Once you stand, hitting is prohibited for the rest of the round.

6. Ace = 11 & Jack, Queen, Kings = 10
